Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

GroupLock ("we", "us", or "our") provides a group scheduling coordination service that uses SMS messaging. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use GroupLock:

  • Phone numbers — provided by the event organizer to identify attendees for scheduling purposes.
  • Names — optionally provided by the organizer to identify attendees.
  • SMS message content — the text messages exchanged between GroupLock and attendees, including availability information provided by attendees.
  • Event details — event names, date ranges, and scheduling preferences entered by the organizer.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to operate the GroupLock scheduling service:

  • To send availability request messages to attendees on behalf of the organizer.
  • To receive, parse, and confirm availability replies from attendees.
  • To determine scheduling overlap and display results to the organizer.
  • To send event confirmation and reminder messages to attendees.

Mobile Messaging Disclosure: Mobile phone numbers and consent are never shared with third parties, affiliates, or used for marketing purposes. Message frequency varies per event (typically 3–8 messages per scheduling cycle). Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for help.

3. Information We Do Not Share

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes or any purpose other than delivering the GroupLock service.

Information is not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third parties.

Mobile phone numbers and SMS consent are never shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following service providers solely to operate the platform:

  • Twilio — for SMS message delivery and receipt. Messages pass through Twilio's infrastructure in accordance with Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — for AI-powered message parsing. Message content is processed by Anthropic's API in accordance with Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — for application hosting and database storage.

These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Data Retention

Message history and availability data is retained only as long as needed to complete the scheduling event. Organizers may delete events and associated data at any time.

6. Opting Out of SMS Messages

Attendees may opt out of receiving SMS messages from GroupLock at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further messages will be sent to that number for any GroupLock event. To re-enable messages, reply START. Reply HELP for assistance.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting us at the address below.
